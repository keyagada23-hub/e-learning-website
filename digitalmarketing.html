Digital Marketing

SEO Cares Website

Overview:

SEO Cares is a company focused on SEO (Search Engine Optimization) and digital marketing. According to their LinkedIn, they help websites rank higher in Google to generate leads and sales. They run SEO training courses, including in-person and video-based training.


Features:
  • Training Course: Their course (e.g., in Lahore) covers SEO from basics to advanced strategies.
  • Practical Learning: Their training includes exercises, quizzes, and downloadable resources to help learners apply what they learn.
  • Flexibility: They offer both online (video) training and offline classroom training.
  • Community: They maintain a WhatsApp group for students to collaborate, ask questions, and get feedback.
  • Career Focus: Their program is designed to help students become SEO practitioners, either to work in SEO or start their own SEO business.
  • Cost-Effectiveness: They emphasize value for money in their courses.
  • Digital Marketing Advice: Their website has articles and guides on how to learn digital marketing, not just SEO.



D&B Placements Website

Overview:

D&B Placements is part of Digital & Beyond, a company that combines recruitment (hiring) services with digital marketing expertise. They support both job seekers and companies, handling talent acquisition as well as digital hiring strategies.

Features:
  • Recruitment & Staffing: Full recruitment services for permanent and contract roles.
  • Talent Acquisition: They work end-to-end with companies, from sourcing candidates to managing the hiring lifecycle.
  • liRecruitment Marketing: They use digital marketing techniques (social media, PPC, email, content) to attract candidates.
  • liTraining & Development: They offer training programs to upskill employees or job seekers.
  • Digital Marketing Services: In addition to recruiting, they help brands with digital marketing (web presence, social media, etc.).
