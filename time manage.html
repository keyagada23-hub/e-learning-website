Alison Website

Overview:

Alison is an online learning platform founded in Ireland. It focuses on offering free courses on learning English language. Courses are available at two levels: Certificate and Diploma.

Duration
  • Courses: Typically take 2–3 hours to complete.
  • Diploma Courses: More in-depth; can take 8–10+ hours or more, depending on the course.
  • No strict time limit — because courses are self-paced, you can pause and resume anytime.

Features
  • Massive Course Catalog: Over thousands of courses across many categories.
  • Self-Paced Learning: No fixed deadlines; learn at your own speed.
  • Assessments: Many courses include assessments; you need to pass to finish.
  • Accreditation: Courses are CPD-accredited (Continuing Professional Development).



Coursera Website

Overview:

Coursera is a large online learning platform founded in 2012 by Stanford professors Andrew Ng and Daphne Koller. It partners with top universities and organizations to offer courses, specializations, professional certificates, and full degree programs.

Duration
  • Guided Projects: ~1–2 hours.
  • Regular Courses: Usually 4–12 weeks, depending on pace and weekly commitment.
  • Specializations / Professional Certificates: Several months (varies by specialization).
    • e5 Features
    • Courses & Specializations: Offers single courses as well as multi-course “Specializations” (with capstone projects).
    • >Guided Projects: Short, hands-on projects (typically 1–2 hours) to build job-relevant skills.
    • Fully online bachelor’s and master’s degrees from accredited universities.

    Free Access Options:
    • Preview Mode: You can access the first module of many courses for free.
    • Financial Aid: Available for learners who need support to pay for full course access + certificate.
    • Full Course, No Certificate: Some courses allow full access WITHOUT certification.
    • Assessments: Depending on course, includes quizzes, peer-reviewed assignments, and sometimes final projects.
    • Flexible Learning: Courses can be time-bound (weekly schedule) or on-demand.
